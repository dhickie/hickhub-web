HickHub does not collect any personally identifiable information about those that use it.

Logging in to Amazon through HickHub allows us to link your Echo device to the HickHub on your local network. We only collect your Amazon User ID for this purpose.

Once your HickHub is linked to your Amazon account, we store your global IP address in order to call the hub with your commands.

We do not share this information with any third parties.

All of this information can be confirmed by viewing the HickHub source code which is publicly available.
